Frequently Asked Questions
  • Q: How will I know when I get accepted or rejected?
  • A: We will send your application to our offices, and you can expect a reply back to your email within 2 business weeks.
  • Q:What do I do when I get the acception email?
  • A: We will specify in the email whether or not we want to meet in person. If we want to meet in person. You will go to the nearest Little Angel’s facility where we will get to know you better.
  • Q: What is different about your Montessori compared to others?
  • A: Compared to other Montessories, our company strives to provide an amazing experience for our little Angels while also making sure our employees enjoy their jobs.
  • Q: How often do spots open up for jobs?
  • A: There is not a set number, but our spots fill up very quickly so I suggest being active on the website and looking frequently.
  • Q: Are there part time jobs offered too?
  • A: There is not a set number, but our spots fill up very quickly so I suggest being active on the website and looking frequently.
  • Q:Are there part time jobs offered too?
  • A: Yes, however you must mention this to our employers beforehand as to negotiate the salary difference during the employment process to reduce chances of accidental leaves and use of PTO.
  • Q: What is the required age to work at Little Angel’s?
  • A: Our jobs are offered to people over the age of 21 with a basic teaching degree, however we also offer leadership programs to 13-20 year olds in order to gain work experience in the education field.
